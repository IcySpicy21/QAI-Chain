\section{Ethics and Broader Impacts}

The scary failure here is not ``model gets mediocre reward''; it is autonomy that looks fine on average but misbehaves under adversarial telemetry or quietly drifts governance knobs. We rely on layered control---uncertainty routing, shielding, auditable logs---but that stack is not a substitute for security review, incident playbooks, or whatever regulator applies to your jurisdiction before you point this at real assets.
