\section{Conclusion}

We like the architecture because it is blunt: learn with PPO, route with uncertainty, clamp with a shield, keep a deterministic branch you trust. Under shift that bundle changes failure modes compared with executing the policy raw, and you can keep the learned block small.

The VQC-style module is only there to buy a nonlinear uncertainty signal cheaply under classical simulation. On-chain logging is optional audit glue. Those roles are separated on purpose so nobody reads ``quantum'' as a performance claim.

Evidence spans the thirty-seed Safety Gym pair, the fifty-seed governance controller tables, calibration and perturbation diagnostics, and the cost appendix. Regeneration scripts and artifacts are available upon request.
